00 / FOUNDATION

Principles

Three commitments that keep everything — from a service card to a whole page — feeling like Synkope.
01

Warm & considered

Cream gradients, soft shadows and a confident orange replace the cold blue-on-white trope of consulting sites. The palette should feel like a Scandinavian autumn, not a server room.

02

Calm density

Plenty of whitespace, a single 1200px column, large body copy and 2‑up grids. Content earns its place; we never pad with filler stats or decorative icons.

03

Expertise, not sales

Copy is declarative and specific (Prince2®, SAFe, EMC standards). We describe what we do for clients; we don't promise magic. The UI follows: no glow, no pop-ups, no hype.

13 / APPLIED

Usage guidelines

Rules of thumb for applying the system consistently.
Topic Do Don't
Orange Do use on a single primary action per screen, and for all headings. Don't use orange for body paragraphs or for large filled regions outside heroes and accent cards.
Blue Do use --s-secondary for all body copy and inline links. Don't use near‑black (#000) or pure gray for text — the blue is the ink.
Gradients Do use the cream section washes to separate long pages. Don't stack orange‑to‑blue brand gradients on anything other than hero imagery and the FAB.
Cards Do leave 40px internal padding and keep copy under 30 words. Don't add icons, illustrations or glow effects — the gradient top‑rule is the only ornament.
Type Do pair Inter headings with Open Sans body. H1 stays on primary; H3 on primary‑light. Don't introduce additional typefaces, condensed styles or italic display.
Copy Do write short, declarative Norwegian. Name the standard (Prince2®, SAFe, NATO EMC). Don't use consulting clichés ("synergy", "best-in-class", "world-class"). Avoid emoji.
Imagery Do use real photography of people and labs; fall back to the brand gradient placeholder. Don't use stock business imagery, AI illustrations or generic circuit‑board visuals.
Motion Do use the 300ms ease transition on color, shadow and subtle translateY lifts. Don't animate on page load beyond a single fade‑in, and disable hover lifts on touch.
